MISSION

Veem's mission is to be the radically new visual voicemail system, which enables mobile users to be able to have more control over their basic voicemail system, by tailoring it to suit their needs. Veem and its smart folders would be an album of memories to share within generations to come. Never be forced to delete a message again. Veem automatically backs up your voicemail for you by connecting with your phone carrier. Save, organize and share your messages with Veem.

VISION

Veem's mission is to become the leader in innovative visual voicemail systems. Veem's diverse abilities will not only be, back up voicemails to its own cloud for its subscribers and to provide smart folders to hold albums of memories, but also to create its own community in which Veem users can check out what other Veem’ers are doing as well as pass their special Veems onto future generations to come. This will be the future of visual voicemail brought to you by the Veem app.

Veem Cloud

Clouds are not just for photos, videos, or documents. Veem cloud auto-saves all voice mail messages for its subscribers and will be up to the subscriber when they should be deleted or pass onto future generations.
