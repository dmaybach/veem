Veem | Visual Voicemail | Technology

Unlike any other voicemail app

Never be forced to delete a message again. Veem automatically backs up your voicemail for you by connecting with your phone carrier. Save, organize and share your messages with Veem. All you need to get started is

  • Your cell phone carrier name

  • Passcode to your voicemails (usually a 4 digit code)

  • Your cell # and vio-la!
